About Vino & Pasta

Our Story

Founded in 1978 by Nonna Lucia, Vino & Pasta began as a tiny kitchen with a big heart. She stirred sauces with love and rolled pasta by hand, just like her mamma taught her. Locals came for the food, but stayed for the warmth and stories at every table.

Over the decades, it became a home for travelers, dreamers, and old souls. At Vino & Pasta, tradition isn’t served — it’s lived.
